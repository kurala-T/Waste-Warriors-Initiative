Competition Results: Live Leaderboard

Grade 6,7 and 8 are competing to be the ultimate Waste Warriors! Winners are chosen based on waste reduction, recycling accuracy, and innovative ideas.

Competition Rules

  • Waste Segregation: Highest accuracy in sorting paper from general waste.
  • Waste Reduction: Greatest percentage decrease in total classroom waste.
  • Innovation: Creative and practical ideas for reducing waste in school.
  • Prizes: The winning class gets a the "Waste Warriors" trophy!
